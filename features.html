Automated Fish Pond Management System


Features


Automated Feeding

• A hopper filled with fish food connects to a servo motor controlled by the
Arduino.
• Pre-programmed feeding times or a light sensor (detecting day/night cycle)
trigger the servo motor to dispense a specific amount of food into the
fishpond.


Water Quality


Sensors continuously monitor key water parameters:
ammonia, nitrate, pH, and dissolved oxygen.


Multi-Sensory Deterrence Mechanisms


Based on sensor data, the system
activates various deterrents:
• High-Powered Strobe Lights: Flashing bright lights to disorient and scare
away predators.
• Automated Water Sprayers: Sudden bursts of water directed towards the
detected predator location.
• Acoustic Alarms and Distress Calls: Emitting predator-specific distress
calls or loud alarm sounds to deter them.
